Our Story

Founded in 2010 by coffee enthusiasts Sarah and Michael Johnson, The Daily Grind began as a small coffee cart in downtown Beanville. What started as a passion project quickly grew into a beloved community hub.

After years of perfecting our craft and listening to our customers, we opened our flagship location on Coffee Street in 2015. Today, we're proud to serve the finest artisanal coffee while supporting local farmers and producers.

Sustainable Sourcing

We partner with ethical farms that practice sustainable agriculture and pay fair wages to workers.

Community Focus

We regularly host local artists, musicians, and fundraisers for neighborhood causes.

Craftsmanship

Every drink is made with care by our professionally trained baristas using premium equipment.
